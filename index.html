sublitr

A streamlined submission manager.

sublitr (sub - lit - er) makes it easy for publications to receive, review, and decide on submissions. It provides a streamlined interface that allows editors to view, comment on, and update the status of submissions from any device, with nothing to download.

Submitters get a unified way of tracking all of their submissions through the review process.
